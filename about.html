ABOUT US

Riding Gear Store

Focused on giving the best purchase experience

Started in 2023, Riders Choice has been striving on giving customer the best possible purchase and after-sales experience.Our mission is to provide world class,high performance & comfortable motorcycling riding gear, all at an affordable price.

How do we do it?

Understanding customer needs

At Riders Choice, we have always been good listeners.This starts from understanding customer needs to what kind of products should be stocked in our portfolio.

Suggestion based on real time experience

We have a team of motorcycle riders, some riding from long time while other not so long. Neverthless our suggestions are always based on real time experiences. We also collate data on product feedback from our large customer base. This means if we suggest any product to a customer it will be based on actual usage of the product.

Explaing products feature

Any product that is stocked at Riders Choice, is well studied & understood. At time we even put many products into actual use to understand better. Thi way, we are able to explain our customers all the product features in detail.
